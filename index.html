Prompt Engineering Tips

Structure & Refinement Strategy

Effective prompts follow this iterative pattern:

  • Subject/Scene: Clear description of the main subject and environment
  • Mood & Atmosphere: Emotional tone, lighting, and ambiance
  • Artistic Style: Specific references to artists, art movements, or mediums
  • Technical Details: Resolution, aspect ratio, camera angles, and technical specifications
  • Refinement: Iterative testing with specific adjustments to control output

Key Takeaway: Show both your initial brief and refined version to demonstrate mastery of prompt engineering. Artist references and technical specifications significantly improve consistency.
